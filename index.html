Peine del viento

The Comb of the Wind is located at the western end of La Concha Bay, at the end of Ondarreta beach, in San Sebastián. It is made up of three of Chillida’s monumental steel sculptures, weighing 10 tons each, embedded in natural rocks rising from the Cantabrian Sea.
